Half-Life: Alyx

‘Half-Life: Alyx’ was a game released in March 2020 and was the first Valve single player title released in 9 years since the release of Portal 2. Half-Life: Alyx is a 'virtual reality only' first person shooter and is one of the most ambitious virtual reality games released to date. The game is set in between the events of Half-Life 1 and 2, and sees the player playing as the the Half Life 2 side character Alyx.

Innovations

After several failed attempts to create another installment in the Half-Life franchise, in 2010 Valve began focusing on Virtual Reality Gaming instead as they believe that virtual reality will be the future of gaming. Valve is now one of the main forerunners in VR, manufactuaring and selling their own VR headset the ‘Valve Index’.



They experimented with many different virtual reality game concepts before deciding that a game set in the half-life universe would work well. Half-Life Alyx is the first of it’s kind and will no doubt inspire other video game companies to enter the virtual world.
