Valve Software

Valve Software is an American video game publisher, developer and digital distributor that has created many critically acclaimed games that have sold millions, such as the ‘Half-Life’ and ‘Portal’ series. Valve Software was founded in 1996 by former Microsoft employees Gabe Newell and Mike Harrington.

Here is a list of the most popular games Valve has created over the years:

  1. Half-Life
  2. Half-Life 2
  3. Half-Life Alyx
  4. Portal
  5. Portal 2
  6. Counter Strike
  7. Left 4 Dead

The company started off with a bang, with their debut title ‘Half-Life’, an action horror first person shooter, selling 10 million copies, which was unheard of at the time for a brand new compnay. This game broke boundaries and innovated new technologies and storytelling methods that inspired a whole new generation of games.

Valve has continued this trend of technological innovation, with each new game they release pioneering new technology. Their newest game ‘Half-Life Alyx’ being a prime example of this, with it being the first 'Virtual Reality only' title made by a major company. In this website, I will showcase the the main games in Valve's main Singleplayer game series 'Half-Life', and talk about how those games innovated and created new technology that continue to influenced the entire video game industry.
